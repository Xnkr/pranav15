PIPS

PRANAV INDIAN PARLIAMENT SIMULATION

Are you someone who is interested in debating? Are you someone who takes an interest in the affairs of the country? Are you a frequent youth parliament participant? Then you're here in the right place, at the right time. It's that time of the year again when students from various streams suit up to become the leaders of India and bring forth their viewpoints on various issues.

PRANAV 2K15 brings to you PIPS- PRANAV INDIAN PARLIAMENT SIMULATION

PIPS is an Indian version Model United Nations (MUN), an event that has immense popularity. In PIPS, each participant is assigned a state and they, as delegates must represent those states in the mock parliamentary sessions conducted. Every year a current issue is taken up and the delegates together with the head must decide whether the bill must be passed. The prizes for the event would be awarded based on the oratorical skill, depth of knowledge, reasoning capability and the overall temperament of the delegate participating.

Rules:

  • • It is an individual event.
  • • A registration fee of 150/- is to be paid.
  • • Participants should report before 9.00am.
  • • The moderators decision is to be considered final and no discrepancy shall be entertained.
  • • The participants must maintain a debate decorum and must be dressed in formals.
  • • Laptops are compulsory.
  • • The participants must adhere to the rules and regulations formed by the symposium committee members and volunteers.
  • • The use of internet during sessions is strictly prohibited.
  • • Lunch will be provided.
